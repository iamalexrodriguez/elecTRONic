Made during Ironhack's Full Time Web Dev course, Mexico City, Jan 2019

  1. Survive! If you crash against the walls, against yourself or your opponent you will award the other player 20 points.

  2. Eat! If you touch the block you win 20 points.

  3. You will play 3 rounds each game.

  4. At the end of those rounds the player with the highest amount of points will win.

  5. Expect a plot twist at the beginning of each round!
